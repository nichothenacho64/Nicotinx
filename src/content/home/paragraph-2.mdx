# What can I offer?

What I like doing most is taking ambiguous research and competing requirements, especially in specialist domains where the content is dense, and turning it all into something coherent. I’m drawn to information-heavy products where the challenge isn’t oversimplifying things, but organising them properly. The interface should reflect the reasoning behind it and have traceable logic, while the design should continue to function even after implementation.

<br />

I want the next person who touches my work to understand why it’s built the way it is without needing to reverse-engineer my decisions.
